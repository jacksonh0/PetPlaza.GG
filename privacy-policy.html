Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/29/24

At PetPlaza, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information in order to provide you with the best experience on our website:

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you register an account with us, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and other contact details.
  • Payment Information: For processing payments, we may collect payment details like credit/debit card information and billing address. This information is processed securely by our third-party payment processors.

Non-Personal Information

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, including your browsing activity, IP address, device information, and usage statistics. This helps us improve our services and website.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. These cookies help us remember your preferences and enable certain features on our site. You can control cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect the functionality of some features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Process transactions and send order confirmations and receipts.
  • Communicate with you about your orders, account updates, and promotions.
  • Respond to your customer support inquiries and requests.
  • Personalize your experience on our website by recommending products and services based on your preferences.
  • Analyze usage trends and enhance the performance of our website.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement a variety of security measures to protect it. This includes using encryption for sensitive data during transmission (SSL) and storing data securely. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers to:

  • Process payments and orders.

  • Send marketing communications (if you have opted in).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage your preferences and privacy settings through your account settings or by contacting us directly.
